How we started...

Welcome to ShopInfiniti, your number one source for all things that you are used to in your day to day life. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of quality products, with a focus on customer satisfaction, customer support, and efficiency. Founded in 2019 by Dev and Meet Patel, ShopInfiniti has come a long way from its beginnings in Toronto, Ontario. When Dev and Meet first started out, their passion for giving customers good quality products for a cheap price drove them to stop studying for other subjects so that ShopInfiniti can offer you the first ever website to sell cars. We now serve customers all over the GTA and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
Sincerely,
Team ShopInfiniti
